About us

What is Dhundlo?

Dhundlo is a multifunction Freelancing application where users can register as a freelance service provider or a customer. This is a platform where every individual can find a service provider which relates to our Basic Home needs, Tech, Vehicles, Cinema, Tutors, etc. A talented individual can register himself to have a second income for himself. The mission of Dhundlo is to change the way people see Freelancers in the real world.

What are the Features?

Here you will be able to find Daily needs Service Providers near you. So, now you don’t need to run everywhere for any kind of service that you need whether it is for your home, Tech, Gadgets, Electronics, Teachers, etc. We are also focused on directly contacting customers to Service Providers where you will not be disturbed by vigorously irritating calls, you will be the one who chooses which service provider you need from a list of service professionals who are ready to serve you based on their ratings, reviews, and experience. This is a free application for all users and Freemium for Service providers.

Team behind Dhundlo

We are a team of professionals who always had the AIM of making a positive change in the society. We are always in search of finding loopholes and solving them until it solves the problem in the real world.
